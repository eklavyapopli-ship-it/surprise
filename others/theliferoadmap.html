Happy Teacher's Day
Riya Didi
Not only in code, but in life’s wide lane,
Ria Didi’s words fell soft as rain.
She drew me maps where dreams could grow,
And taught me paths I ought to know.

Her wisdom flowed, both calm and deep,
A compass to guide, a flame to keep.
Through storms of doubt, she showed the ground,
Where strength is steady, and hope is found.

Not just from books, her lessons came,
But from the heart, a brighter flame.
Her voice still echoes when I choose,
Reminding me of roads to use.

Thank you, Ria Didi,
For guidance that shaped not just my code, but my soul
