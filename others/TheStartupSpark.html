The Startup Spark
Happy Teacher's Day
Ishika Didi
A vision flickered, fragile, small,
A dream to build, to stand, to call.
Yet fear would whisper, doubts would stay,
How could I pitch? What words to say?

Then Ishika Didi, strong and bright,
Stepped in and set my path alight.
Her wisdom sharpened every part,
Her faith ignited my beating heart.

With her support, I took the stage,
My voice grew bold, my thoughts engaged.
And when they heard, they chose to see,
A spark of hope, a chance in me.

Thank you, Ishika Didi,
For lighting the fire that fuels my startup soul.
